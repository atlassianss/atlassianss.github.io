rabbit监控模板

Created2021-09-24|Updated2021-09-24
|Post View:

rabbitmq metrics
节点统计：rabbitmq_running
消息总数：rabbitmq_queue_messages_total{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
rabbitmq_queue_messages
交换： rabbitmq_exchangesTotal{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
通道： rabbitmq_channelsTotal{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
消费者： rabbitmq_consumersTotal{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
接连： rabbitmq_connectionsTotal{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
队列： rabbitmq_queuesTotal{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
消息/队列：rabbitmq_queue_messages
消息/主机：sum by (vhost)(rabbitmq_queue_messages_ready)
sum by (vhost)(rabbitmq_queue_messages_published_total)
sum by (vhost)(rabbitmq_queue_messages_delivered_total)
sum by (vhost)(rabbitmq_queue_messages_unacknowledged)
内存：rabbitmq_node_mem_used{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
rabbitmq_node_mem_limit{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
磁盘：rabbitmq_node_disk_free{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
rabbitmq_node_disk_free_limit{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
文件描述符：rabbitmq_fd_used{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
rabbitmq_fd_total{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
套接字描述符：rabbitmq_sockets_used{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
rabbitmq_sockets_total{instance=“^$addr”,job=”RabbitMQ”}
